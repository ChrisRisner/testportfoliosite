About

My name is Chris and I'm a (very) amateur photographer located in Seattle, Washington. I've always been interested in photography and decided to start sharing some of my pictures online.

I've been shooting on Nikon cameras for about a decade now. My first was a Nikon D3500 DSLR, followed by a Z50. I currently shoot with a Z8. For my phone camera, I'm currently using a Pixel 10 Pro XL.

This site is a more or less blatant rip off of Stephan Morais' Photography site.
